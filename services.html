Our Simple 4-Step Process

1

Contact & Consultation

Reach out via phone, text, or our online form. We'll discuss your travel timeline, determine which service level you need, and provide a clear quote. Our consultations are free and informative.

2

Document Collection & Review

We'll provide you with a detailed checklist of required documents. Once you send them to us, our team carefully reviews everything to ensure accuracy and completeness before submission.

3

Expedited Submission & Processing

Our authorized representatives will personally submit your application at the appropriate passport agency. We navigate the bureaucracy so you don't have to, ensuring the fastest possible processing.

4

Secure Delivery to Your Door

Once your passport is ready, we ship it directly to you via insured express delivery with full tracking. You'll know exactly when to expect your passport to arrive.

Typical Documents Required

Proof of U.S. Citizenship

Certified birth certificate, naturalization certificate, or previous passport. Original documents or certified copies are required.

Government-Issued ID

Valid driver's license, state ID, or military ID. Must be current and include a photograph.

Passport Photo

Recent color photo meeting Department of State specifications. We can take this for you at our office.

Completed Application

Form DS-11 (new passport) or DS-82 (renewal). We can help you complete this correctly.

Travel Itinerary

Proof of upcoming travel plans if requesting expedited processing. Flight confirmations or hotel bookings work well.

Payment

Government fees plus our service fee. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.
